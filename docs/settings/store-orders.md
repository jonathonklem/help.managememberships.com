# Store Orders

The **Store Orders** section allows you to view and manage orders placed through your portal's product store. You can filter orders, track fulfillment status, and manage shipping details.

---

## Filtering Orders

Use the filters at the top of the orders list:

### Search
Search by customer name, email, or tracking number.

### Status
Filter orders by payment status:
- **Paid** - Payment completed
- **Unfulfilled** - Paid but not yet shipped or picked up
- **Fulfilled** - Order has been completed
- **Pending** - Awaiting payment
- **Failed** - Payment failed
- **Refunded** - Payment was refunded

### Fulfillment Type
Filter by how the order will be delivered:
- **Local Pickup** - Customer picks up in person
- **Shipping** - Order is shipped to the customer

---

## Fulfilling Orders

When an order is ready to be fulfilled, you can mark it as complete and optionally add a **Tracking Number** for shipped orders.
