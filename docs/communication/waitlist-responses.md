# Waitlist Responses

The **Waitlist Responses** section shows all submissions from waitlist interest forms embedded in your blog posts and pages using the `{{topic}}` tag syntax.

---

## Overview

When visitors or members fill out a waitlist interest form on your portal, their responses are collected here. Each response includes:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | The respondent's name |
| **Email** | Their email address |
| **Phone** | Their phone number |
| **Topic** | The interest topic they signed up for (matches the tag used in the blog post or page) |
| **Message** | Any message they included |
| **Created At** | When the response was submitted |

---

## Filtering

Use the **Topic** filter to narrow responses to a specific interest area. Topics are automatically populated based on the tags you've used in your content.

---

## Using Waitlist Data

Waitlist responses can be targeted directly from **Text Campaigns** and **Email Campaigns** using the Wait Lists option, allowing you to send messages to people interested in specific topics.
