# Waiver Compliance

The **Waiver Compliance** report helps you track which members have signed required agreements and identify compliance gaps, especially around bookings and activities.

---

## Time Period

Filter the report by time period:
- **Last 24 Hours**
- **Last 7 Days** (default)
- **Last 30 Days**
- **Custom** - Specify a custom date range

---

## Report Data

The report shows compliance information for members who have participated in activities during the selected time period, including:
- Whether required agreements have been signed
- Members with outstanding waivers
- Agreement status relative to bookings and check-ins
