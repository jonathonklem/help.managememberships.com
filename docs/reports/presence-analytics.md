# Presence Analytics

The **Presence Analytics** report provides insights into member visit patterns by combining data from check-ins, door access logs, and GPS activity. It compares this week's activity against last week to identify trends.

---

## Metrics

- **Unique Visitors** - Number of distinct members who visited (this week vs last week)
- **Average Dwell Time** - How long members typically stay
- **Total Activities** - Combined check-ins, door scans, and GPS-detected visits

---

## Chart View

A visual comparison chart shows daily activity for the current week overlaid against the previous week, making it easy to spot patterns and changes in member behavior.

---

## Activity Breakdown

The report breaks down activity by data source:
- **GPS Only** - Members detected via GPS proximity
- **Door Only** - Members who scanned in at a door
- **Full Participation** - Members who checked in to classes or activities
